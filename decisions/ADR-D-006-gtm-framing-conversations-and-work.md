# ADR-D-006 — "Gap between conversations and work" adopted as canonical GTM framing

## Context
Multiple GTM framings were in play: "AI for HR," "AI meeting notes for managers," "performance enablement automation." Each pulled toward different positioning and different feature priorities. Paul (GTM) proposed in a design Group DM: lead with "the gap between conversations and work" — the conversations are happening; the problem is that nothing from them lands on the performance record. This centers the customer problem rather than the product capability.

## Decision
Adopt **"the gap between conversations and work"** as the canonical GTM framing. All Phase-1 messaging, sales positioning, and PRD GTM sections inherit this. The story is about routing what's already being said into the record that drives reviews, raises, and growth — not about AI.

## Consequences
- PRD §1 Problem statements across the trio open with this framing.
- Sales motion: "Your managers are already having the 1:1s. We just route them into Betterworks."
- Differentiation: vs Otter/Fireflies/Zoom AI Companion (summarization-only), Betterworks routes signal to the performance record.
- Feature priorities derive from this: anything that doesn't close the conversation→record gap is deprioritized.
