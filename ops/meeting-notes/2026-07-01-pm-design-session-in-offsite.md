# PM <> Design Session in offsite — 2026-07-01

## Summary
Lalit and Gauri planned an AI session for a small combined PM + design audience (~6-7 people, excluding US team and Rinku) at the upcoming off-site hackathon. - Standardizing AI workflows across PM and design is a valid topic but should not dominate.
