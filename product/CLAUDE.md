# Product

## What's here
- `specs/` — product specs (use `.claude/skills/write-spec/SKILL.md` for new ones)
- `roadmap/` — Now/Next/Later and quarterly roadmap docs
- `research/` — customer interview notes, synthesis outputs, raw transcripts in `research/raw/`

## Rules
- Always check `specs/` before drafting a new spec — may already exist
- For stakeholder-facing updates, follow `.claude/skills/stakeholder-update/SKILL.md`
- Research synthesis: drop transcripts in `research/raw/`, then synthesize-themes
